Boundary Waters

Exploration

The Boundary Waters is a large area of federal land that has been set aside to preserve the wilderness. It is a sanctuary for animals and people a like. There are no perminate structures allowed and it is the closest thing we can get to in our civilized world to being alone and shut off. To me, it is the way I would like to spend my vacations. Although it can be alot of work it is the kind of envronment that really makes me feel like I belong. If you are thinking about venturing out into the BWCA here are a few things you may want to have first:

  • Lightweight canoe
  • Large Pack (duluthpack)
  • Fishing gear
  • Knowledge and Experience with outdoor survival
  • Lightweight cookware
  • Tent
  • Sleeping bag
